Sr. Control Systems Engineer

2018-present

Yesterday, 3D printing was limited to prototyping applications; but, today, it is one big step ahead and is the way to digital fabrication at large scales. With this breakthrough technology, parts can be printed 100-400 times faster compared to conventional 3D printing methods.
